Global Guardian Framework Tools

Practical resources to implement animal welfare transformation through community-led guardianship

These tools and templates are designed to help you implement the Global Guardian Framework in various contexts. From establishing community welfare assessments to creating guardian rights charters and building alternative protein transitions, these resources provide step-by-step guidance for transforming animal welfare systems through guardianship rather than ownership.
